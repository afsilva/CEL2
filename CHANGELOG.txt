Cloud Engine Light for iPhone

- Original Cloud Engine Light app was developed for Android Devices: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.andersonsilva.cel
- As of 2012/12/26 Cloud Engine Light for iPhone supports:
  . Links to Openshift's status page
  . Links to social media sites like twitter, facebook and google plus
  . Background services that checks the status of Openshift every 30 minutes
  . If an issue is open with Openshift, notification is published on your iPhone.

- version 1.5.2-dev
